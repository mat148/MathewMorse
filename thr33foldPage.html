The Fold

Over the summer of 2014 I had the opportunity to work with a group of awesome people at Thr33fold, an Interactive and Digital Agency. I was a creative intern and I worked on things such as a promotional video, web / mobile design, web development, and social media design. I had one of the best summers of my life and I'm extreemly thankful for being able to work with them. On top of the fun in the office, we also did things out of the office, such as swing dancing, Rock band, and hanging out outside of work hours. From the second I arrived I felt like I was a part of the family. It was an amazing experience and I learned so much from them.

I was unable to finish the prototype completely but I learned a lot. Looking back on it now I wished I had used Jquery and that I used a mobile framework.
