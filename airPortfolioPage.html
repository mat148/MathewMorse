About The Project

The goal of this project was to create an interactive way to visualize data. It was a exploration in potential ways that data can be displayed beyond just listing raw data. I created a app mockup and an interactive prototype to demonstrate my idea.

Project Concept

AIR is a mobile experience that allows home owners to monitor the air quality within their home and protect their family.

Timeline

Conception

Thinking about what data I wanted to work with and how to convey that data. Looking at inspiration and other data visualizations.

Competitive Research

Looking at applications that currently exist in the market so I can figure out what they did right or wrong and improve on those ideas.

Sketches

Thinking with my hands to work out interactions and layout.

Three Variations & Moodboards

Came up with three different directions for the look & feel of the application. I wanted to make sure the user felt a certain way.

Prototype

The creation of an interactive prototype in html, css, and Javascript to demonstrate the interactions.

Research

Researching the specific data that my user would want and need to see in the application.

Personas

Understanding the users that would use the application.

Wireframes / Interactions

Creating low & high fidelity wire-frames so the layout and interactions are clearly laid out.

Final Design

A combination of all the previous steps into a final design.

    User Persona

  • Age 30 and older
  • Male or female
  • College level education

Users would use AIR to monitor the air quality within their home and provide a safe environment for their loved ones.

Sketches

Wireframes

With the wire-frames I was trying to get an understanding of how the app flowed and how some of the animations happen. After doing this I had a solid idea of how everything worked.

Moodboard

With the mood board I was going for a comfortable, homey, and simple feeling. I wanted the user to feel safe and comfortable.

Final Design

Prototype

For this project we were tasked to create a prototype to show how the application flowed. I decided that I wanted to program my app with html,css, and javascript. I was unable to finish the prototype completely but I learned a lot. Looking back on it now I wished I had used Jquery and that I used a mobile framework. But I am glad that I attempted it, this helped me better understand javascript.

Link to the prototype

Final Thoughts

This project was a long and hard process that challenged me to think about the user and how the interactions would happen. In the end I was happy with the way the data was presented in a more interesting way.
