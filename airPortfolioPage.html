About The Project

The goal of this project was to create a interactive way to visualize data. It was a exploration in potential ways that data can be displayed beyond just listing raw data. I created a app mockup and a interactive prototype to demonstrate my idea.

Project Concept

AIR is a mobile experience that allows home owners to monitor the air quality within their home and protect their family.

Timeline

    User Persona

  • Age 30 and older
  • Male or female
  • College level education

Users would use AIR to monitor the air quaility within their home and provide a safe enviroment for their loved ones.

Sketches

Wireframes

With the wireframes I was trying to get a understanding of how the app flowed and how some of the animations happend. After doing this I had a solid idea of how everything worked.

Moodboard

With the mood board I was going for a comfortable, homey, and simple feeling. I wanted the user to feel safe and comfortable.

Final Design

Prototype

For this project we were tasked to create a prototype to show how the application flowed. I decided that I wanted to program my app with html,css, and javascript. I was unable to finish the prototype completely but I learned a lot. Looking back on it now I wished I had used Jquery and that I used a mobile framework. But I am glad that I attempted it, this helped me better understand javascript.

Final Thoughts

This project was a long and hard process that challenged me to think about the user and how the interactions would happen. In the end I was happy with the way the data was presented in a more interesting way.
